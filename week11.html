In the past week and a half, we made the decision to venture away from GotP being a direct combat game, and we chose to focus more on the educational style. For example, Ryan developed a base level with Napoleon. After Napoleon talking about his backstory, the user has to answer 4 questions about what was said, them being his weaponry, the countries he battled, when he died, and his final battle. After answering all 4 questions correctly, the user is returned back to the hub world in Ancient Egypt.

Some of the main features include: a portal to take you from the hub world to levels such as Napoleon-Era France, multiple choice questions with correct answers that are mandatory to move on to the next one, dialogue, win/lose screen, button that returns user to the hub.
