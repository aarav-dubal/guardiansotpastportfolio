History Game Portfolio

Project Description

Guardians of the Past is a game designed to teach children problem-solving skills while incorporating historical education. By reimagining historical figures, the game introduces players to key moments and individuals from history. Each historical figure will include unique facts, and players will be required to answer history-related questions to progress, blending fun and learning seamlessly.

Level Ideas

For every level/time period, give the new character some sort of weapon or mechanic based on the situation

    Ancient Egypt

  • Sneak mode: Makes enemies unaware of where you are and allows you to sneak up on them.
  • Have the character try to sneak into a Pharaoh's tomb and steal the treasure inside while also having cutscenes and dialogue about past pharaohs.

    • Fall of Rome

  • Time Stop: Allows time to freeze or go into slow motion for a short amount of time allowing you to become more powerful.
  • Have the character attempt to stop the Fall of Rome and keep Rome afloat. We will also have the character meet past leaders.

    • 10th Century China

  • Explosives/Smokes: Allows you to place traps in order to help defeat other enemies while smokes will distract enemies and allow you to get away.
  • This section will have your character help with the creation of guns and gunpowder in general. We will have them do this in order to take over China.

    • Black Plague

  • Medicine: Through the level you will acquire more medicines which will allow you to gain new magical abilities or heal yourself.
  • Place them into a level where we have them get specific items in order to create a remedy to cure people from the black plague.

    • 1492

  • Mapping/Scouting: This allows you to enter a first person view almost like controlling a drone to fly ahead and see enemies or traps ahead of you.
  • Have the person help to build and set up new lands as well as have them have a portion where they have to stay alive and help others on the trip.
