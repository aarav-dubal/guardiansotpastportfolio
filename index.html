History Game Portfolio

Project Description

Our vision is to make a third person game where someone goes back in time to different times(levels) and lives through historical events while having a mission to complete. Game will play similar to Assassin's Creed or God of War.

Project Timeline

  • Week 1-3: Research historical figures, develop character concepts, plan storyline and educational goals.
  • Week 4-5: Get familiar with Unreal Engine, create initial sprites, begin scripting educational dialogue and history-related questions.
  • Week 6-8: Program core mechanics such as player interactions, questions for progression, and battle sequences. Design first level/area of the game, focusing on one historical era. Conduct initial playtesting and gather feedback
  • Week 9-12: Add other regions/time periods if time permits. Refine based on dialogue and test thoroughly to make sure quality is maximized
  • End of Semester: Complete and package the game, focusing on delivering a polished demo.
