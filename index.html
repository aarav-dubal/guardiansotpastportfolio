Guardians of the Past

Project Description

Guardians of the Past is an educational game blending historical storytelling with action and puzzle-solving. Players meet reimagined historical figures and tackle challenges that test their knowledge, creativity, and decision-making. The game engages learners through immersive experiences and problem-solving mechanics across eras.

Level Ideas by Era

Ancient Egypt

Sneak Mode: Infiltrate a Pharaoh's tomb and uncover ancient secrets.

Fall of Rome

Time Stop: Slow down time to influence key moments in Roman history.

10th Century China

Explosives & Smokes: Invent gunpowder and outsmart enemies with stealth tools.

The Black Plague

Healing Powers: Use medicinal abilities to save lives and stop the spread.

1492 Exploration

Scouting View: First-person map scanning and surviving uncharted lands.
