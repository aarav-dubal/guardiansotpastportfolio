Guardians of the Past

A History Game Teaching Through Time

Project Description

Guardians of the Past is a game crafted to blend historical education with engaging gameplay. Players explore time periods, interact with key historical figures, and use unique mechanics to solve challenges rooted in real events.

MVP Planning

Level Ideas

  • Ancient Egypt: Sneak mode to infiltrate tombs and recover artifacts while learning about pharaohs.
  • Fall of Rome: Time Stop to alter outcomes and meet historic Roman leaders.
  • 10th Century China: Use explosives and traps, contributing to the invention of gunpowder.
  • Black Plague: Heal with mystical medicines and discover remedies to save civilians.
  • 1492 Era: Scout new lands, survive voyages, and chart unknown territories with a first-person mapping mechanic.
