MVP Checklist

Essential

- At least one Ancient Egyptian figure to fight against

Gods/other historical figures from Ancient Egypt

- Arena to fight in

Coliseum

- Start page/finish screen

- Main character

An Egyptian peasant, once homeless on the streets, who fights his way up the social ladder to build a better life.

Nice to have

- Interactive overworld NPCs

Can talk to NPCs to recieve advice, free items, or side quests.

- SFX

- Level-up system with XP gained from activities

Winning a battle or completing a quest will help you gain XP, and with each level up, you unlock new items, such as skins, abilities, weapons, or currency (if added to game).

Extra

- Additional fighting styles

- Minigames in the overworld

Some interactions with NPC could lead to the user playing various minigames, which could also lead to acquiring new items.

- Currency to spend on items, skins, fighting styles. Earned by defeating opponents.
